Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 28, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

Cookies help us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Provide relevant advertisements
  • Analyze website performance

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

Essential These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

Analytics These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They allow us to improve website performance and user experience.

2.3 Advertising Cookies

Advertising These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

2.4 Functionality Cookies

Functionality These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies on your device when you visit our website:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • Google DoubleClick: For ad targeting and personalization

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their cookie policies:

  • Google Cookie Policy
  • Google AdSense Policy

5. How to Manage Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Please note that removing or blocking cookies may impact your user experience and parts of our website may no longer be fully accessible.

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

5.2 Opt-Out of Third-Party Cookies

You can opt out of third-party cookies by visiting:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Google Ads Settings

6. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner that allows you to:

  • Accept All Cookies: Allow all types of cookies for optimal experience
  • Accept Essential Only: Only allow essential cookies required for website functionality
  • Manage Preferences: Customize which types of cookies you accept

Your cookie preferences will be saved and respected on subsequent visits.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our use of cookies, applicable laws, or other reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
