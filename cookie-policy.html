Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences and understand usage patterns.

2. Why open front Uses Cookies

  • Essential functions: To keep pages working correctly and remember basic preferences such as language choice.
  • Performance: To understand how visitors use the site and improve load speed, navigation, and content discovery.
  • Analytics: To measure page views, traffic sources, and engagement trends.

3. Types of Cookies

TypePurpose
EssentialRequired for core site behavior such as basic navigation and preference storage.
AnalyticsUsed to understand visits, page performance, and interaction patterns.
PreferenceUsed to remember settings such as your selected language on open front pages.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may reduce functionality on certain pages.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us using the details on the Contact Us page.
