Welcome to open front - a fast-paced multiplayer real-time strategy game where dozens of players battle for map dominance. Conquer territories, build cities, construct ports, and forge alliances. Victory comes when you control 72% of the map.

WHAT IS OPEN FRONT?

open front is a fast-paced multiplayer real-time strategy game where dozens of players fight for map dominance in real time. You begin with a small base and expand by conquering neighbors, constructing cities, walls, and ports, and balancing your resources.

As a browser strategy game, open front combines classic territory conquest gameplay with modern multiplayer mechanics. Forge alliances or drop bombs to outmaneuver rivals, battling across both land and sea. Every tactical decision is critical in your quest for total domination.

OPEN FRONT GAMEPLAY

In open front, you start by selecting a position on the map. From this small base, you expand by clicking on adjacent territories. But expansion isn't without risk - each expansion spreads your military thin, making existing territories more vulnerable to attack.

The balance between expansion and defense is the key to success. You need to balance population between troops and workers, make choices between economic development and military investment. Every decision affects your chances of victory.

OPEN FRONT BUILDINGS

  • City: Increases maximum population by 25,000 - essential for building a powerful empire
  • Defense Post: Makes it more difficult for enemies to invade your territory, slows enemy attacks, and increases enemy losses
  • Port: Essential for naval power and trade - allows building Warships and trading with other ports
  • Missile Silo: Launches devastating atomic, hydrogen, or MIRV bombs on enemies (right-click to use)
  • SAM Launcher: Intercepts enemy missiles with different success rates depending on bomb type

OPEN FRONT UNITS

  • Warship: Powerful naval unit to attack coastal territories - patrols water and intercepts enemy trade ships
  • Destroyer: Captures enemy trade ships and generates gold - requires a port
  • Atom Bomb: Causes localized destruction to enemy territories
  • Hydrogen Bomb: More powerful and destructive than Atom Bomb - hits larger areas
  • MIRV: Splits to hit multiple points, affecting only the targeted player

OPEN FRONT ALLIANCES

  • Form Alliances: Send or receive troops and gold to help allies under attack or building up strength
  • Trade Routes: Control ports to establish trade routes for passive gold income
  • Betrayal: Betraying an ally ends the alliance, pauses trade for a few minutes, and marks you as a traitor for 30 seconds
  • Diplomacy: Bots and human players react differently to your actions - diplomacy matters in achieving victory

OPEN FRONT KEY FEATURES

  • Real-Time Multiplayer: Up to 143 players per lobby in intense real-time battles
  • Map Conquest: Strategic map expansion gameplay - control 72% to win
  • Land & Sea Warfare: Build cities, walls, ports, and deploy naval forces
  • Resource Management: Balance troops, workers, and gold for optimal growth
  • Alliance System: Form alliances, establish trade routes, or betray rivals
  • Missile Warfare: Deploy Atom Bombs, Hydrogen Bombs, and MIRVs for devastating attacks
  • Global Leaderboards: Compete with players worldwide by land, troops, and gold
  • Cross-Platform: Play on Browser, Mobile, and Steam

OPEN FRONT CONTROLS

  • Move Camera: W, A, S, D
  • Zoom: Q / E or Scroll Wheel
  • Select & Attack: Left Click to select, drag to attack adjacent territories
  • Radial Menu: Right Click to open build menu, attack, send boats, or request alliances
  • Center Camera: C - center camera on your territory
  • Alternate View: Space - toggle between terrain and country view
  • Attack Ratio: Shift + Left Click or Bottom Left Slider to adjust troops sent
  • Quick Chat: Use preset messages and emojis to communicate with allies and opponents
