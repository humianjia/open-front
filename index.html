Open Front is a fast-paced multiplayer real-time strategy browser game where players fight for territory, build cities, form alliances, and race to control 72% of the map.

What is open front?

open front is a real-time strategy .io-style browser game focused on map conquest, economic growth, and tactical expansion.

Every match starts with a small territory. From there, you expand, defend borders, manage workers and troops, and react to human players and bots in real time.

How to win in open front

  • Expand smartly: Capture nearby land, but do not overextend your military and leave your borders exposed.
  • Grow your economy: Build cities and keep enough workers alive so your empire can keep scaling.
  • Control the sea: Ports, warships, and trade routes create pressure far beyond your borders.
  • Use diplomacy: Alliances can protect you, but betrayal and timing often decide the match.
  • Reach 72%: The main victory condition is controlling 72% of the map before your rivals do.

Core buildings and weapons

  • City: Raises your maximum population and lets your empire support larger armies.
  • Defense Post: Slows enemy invasions and increases the cost of attacking your territory.
  • Port: Unlocks naval pressure, sea travel, and profitable trade routes.
  • Missile Silo: Launches devastating bombs against enemy territory when the timing is right.
  • SAM Launcher: Intercepts incoming missiles and helps protect your late-game infrastructure.

Why players search for open front

  • Real-time strategy depth: open front combines territory expansion, economy management, and military timing in every match.
  • Large multiplayer battles: You can compete against large lobbies where alliances and betrayals matter.
  • Browser-first convenience: You can jump into open front instantly without a heavy download or install process.
  • Replayable matches: Different spawns, different neighbors, and different alliances keep every game fresh.

open front controls

  • Move camera: W, A, S, D
  • Zoom: Q / E or mouse wheel
  • Select and attack: Left click to select, then drag toward adjacent territory.
  • Radial menu: Right click to build, attack, send boats, or manage diplomacy.
  • Center camera: Press C to focus on your territory.
  • Alternate view: Press Space to switch between terrain and country view.
  • Attack ratio: Hold Shift and left click, or use the slider to adjust troop commitment.
  • Quick chat: Use preset messages and emojis to communicate during battle.
