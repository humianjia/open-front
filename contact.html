Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Open Front - FrontWars.io free to play?

A: Yes! All our games are completely free to play directly in your browser. No downloads or payments required.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

A: You can play most games without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save progress, track statistics, and participate in leaderboards.

Q: Can I play on mobile devices?

A: Yes! Our games are designed to work on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices with a modern web browser.

Q: How do I report inappropriate content or behavior?

A: Please contact us immediately at humianjia1995@163.com with details. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

Q: Are there ads in the games?

A: Yes, we display advertisements to support our free service. These ads help us continue providing free games to everyone.
